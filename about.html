About Us

PVP Siddhartha Institute of Technology (PVPSIT) is a prestigious institution known for its academic excellence and state-of-the-art facilities. Located in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, it provides quality education in engineering and technology. Established in 1998, the institute is dedicated to fostering intellectual growth and technological innovation, offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. PVPSIT is recognized for its industry-oriented approach and holistic development programs aimed at producing skilled professionals ready to excel in the global tech landscape.
